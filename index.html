Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival

August 5th - August 8th

Where the Magic & Romance Awaits...

Whether you're married with children, a young couple, or just looking for an escape, we've got you covered.

Hosted at the famous Brooklyn Bridge Park and projected onto 3 extra large viewing screens, our movies will leave you and your family with a smile on your face and a glow in your heart.

All daily programmed films featured from 6pm till midnight, so come when time permits, find your seat and let the magic fill your heart.
